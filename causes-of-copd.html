What causes
COPD?

Exposure to lung irritants
Breathing substances which irritate the lungs like kitchen smoke, chemical fumes and dust.

Mosquito coils used in homes to get rid of mosquitoes are another source of exposure in Indian homes.
Burning a single mosquito coil emits particulate matter equivalent to the amount emitted by a 100 cigarettes.
(Source; J Assoc Physicians India. 2012 Feb, 60 suppl; 5-7, Environ health perspect. 2003, 111; 1454-1460).

COPD can be genetic. (Passed from parents to children)
