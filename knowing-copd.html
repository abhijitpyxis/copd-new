What do i need to know about
COPD?

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is a disease of the Lungs- the organ through which we breathe.

C for Chronic means long term
O for Obstructive means partially blocked
P for Pulmonary means related to lungs
D for Disease means illness

It is a group of diseases that cause obstruction of airflow to the lungs. It is chronic (long term) in nature, and therefore the airflow obstruction is usually permanent.
