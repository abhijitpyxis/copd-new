How do I know if i have
COPD?

Look out for these warning signs:

Persistent (ongoing) cough for a long time. Cough usually worsens in the morning for smokers suffering from COPD.

Excess production of mucus (a thick, jelly-like substance produced by the respiratory system)

Difficulty in breathing

Feeling tired, especially when exercising or doing daily activities

Tightness in the chest

Sleep disturbances

Kindly note that the warning signs could mean other diseases too.
Hence only your Doctor can confirm a COPD diagnosis.
