Exercise

Physical activity is recommended for all COPD patients since it improves physical strength, breathing efficiency and tolerance. Your doctor will recommend both aerobic and resistance exercises.

  • Always consult your doctor before starting a COPD exercise programme.
  • If the COPD symptoms worsen while you exercise, stop immediately and contact your doctor.
